Big days feel lighter when broken into small steps. Use this daily checklist to plan priorities, avoid forgetting tasks, and end each day with a sense of progress.

## What's inside

- Morning priorities & top 3 tasks
- Deep work blocks

- Evening review & plan for tomorrow
