## Why use this checklist

SEO has many moving parts. This checklist helps you audit any page methodically so you don't miss the basics that drive rankings.

## What's inside

- **On-page:** title, meta description, headings, alt text
- **Content:** keyword targeting, internal links
- **Technical:** speed, mobile-friendliness, sitemap, indexing
- **Off-page:** backlinks, listings
