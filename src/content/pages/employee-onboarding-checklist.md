## Why use this checklist

A structured onboarding process helps new employees feel welcome and become productive faster. Standardize it so every hire gets the same great start.

## What's inside

- **Before day 1:** offer letter, accounts, workstation
- **Day 1:** welcome, tour, buddy assignment
- **Week 1:** training, policies, first 1:1
- **Documents:** ID proof, bank details, agreements
