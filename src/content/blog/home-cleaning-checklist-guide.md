A clean home feels great — and it's easier when you have a plan. Here's a simple room-by-room approach.

## Kitchen

Wipe counters, clean the sink, and take out the trash daily; deep-clean the fridge weekly.

## Bathroom

Disinfect surfaces, scrub the toilet, and replace towels regularly.

## Bedrooms & living areas

Dust, vacuum, and change bed linens weekly.
