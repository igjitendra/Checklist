Packing is where most trip-stress begins. A few habits (and a good checklist) fix that.

## 1. Make a list before you touch a bag

Decide *what* you need before you start stuffing things in.

## 2. Roll clothes, don't fold

Rolling saves space and reduces wrinkles.

## 3. Keep documents in one pouch

Passport, tickets, and ID together — always.

## 4. Pack a mini first-aid + medicines kit

Small, but a lifesaver.
