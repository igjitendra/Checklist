Great weddings are planned in stages. Here's a simple timeline to keep everything moving.

## 6+ months before

Set your budget, pick a date, and book the venue.

## 3–6 months before

Send invitations, finalize catering, and choose outfits.

## 1 month before

Confirm vendors, build the day-of timeline, and relax.
