## Why use this checklist

Packing stress is real. This checklist breaks your trip prep into clear sections so nothing gets left behind — whether it's a weekend getaway or a two-week vacation.

## What's inside

- **Documents:** passport, tickets, ID, insurance
- **Electronics:** phone, chargers, power bank, adapter
- **Toiletries:** brush, paste, skincare, medicines
- **Clothing:** day-wise outfits, innerwear, footwear
- **Extras:** snacks, water bottle, umbrella

> Tip: Download the printable PDF, tick items off as you pack, and reuse it for every trip.
