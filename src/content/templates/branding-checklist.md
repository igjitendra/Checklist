## Why use this checklist

Building a brand involves a lot of moving parts. This checklist walks you through every essential element so your brand looks consistent and professional everywhere.

## What's inside

- **Identity:** logo design, brand colors, typography
- **Voice:** brand voice, tagline, brand story
- **Assets:** business cards, packaging, email signature, website
- **Strategy:** brand mission, vision, personality
- **Presence:** social media handles, brand guidelines

> Download the printable **PDF** or a high-quality **JPG image** and tick items off as you build your brand.
